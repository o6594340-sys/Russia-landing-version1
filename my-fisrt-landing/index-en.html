About Us

Built by someone who
fell in love with Moscow.

It was November. Ahmed was in Moscow again — one of many trips over the years. They were having dinner, the kind that stretches long because the conversation doesn't want to stop. Outside, light snow was falling. The streets were glowing — Moscow decorates itself with a confidence that surprises people every time.

Ahmed looked out and said: "Olga. Arabs should see this. I think Russia is an unbelievably interesting destination."

Olga didn't need convincing.

She had already hosted Japanese guests at the highest level in Moscow and St. Petersburg — the kind of visit where nothing is left to impression. She had organised a private programme for a group of wealthy Syrian women, understood the details that matter, the things you only know from experience.

So they built Tailor Studio. Not a tourism company that happens to serve Arabs — a company built from a November dinner, because someone who knew both worlds looked out at a snowy Moscow street and thought: this should be shared.

FAQ

Answers to questions
from Arab agencies

Do you have Arabic-speaking guides in all three cities? +

Yes. Our certified guides speak Arabic in Moscow, St. Petersburg and Sochi. Female guides are available for women's groups and families. All guides hold state accreditation and have experience working with Arab tourists.

How do you ensure halal dining? +

We work exclusively with restaurants that hold halal certification or reliably meet the standards. All meals in our programmes are arranged at vetted venues. We provide a list of partner restaurants on request. During Ramadan we arrange Suhoor and Iftar.

How does visa support work? +

We prepare a full documentation package for a Russian visa for every tourist in the group. We know the specific requirements for nationals of the UAE, Qatar, Jordan, Saudi Arabia and Kuwait. The process typically takes 5–10 business days.

What are the payment terms for agencies? +

Flexible B2B terms: 30% deposit on confirmation, the balance 14 days before the group arrives. We accept payment in USD and AED. For regular partners — individual terms and net rates with 15–20% commission.

What is the minimum group size you accept? +

We work with individual travellers (FIT) as well as groups from 2 persons. For larger groups (30+) — special terms and a dedicated escort manager.

What if a client has a problem during the trip? +

Our manager is available on WhatsApp 24/7 throughout the entire trip. We respond within 15 minutes. We have established contacts with medical services and emergency resources in all three cities. In 12 years of operation, not a single client has been left without assistance.

Can the programme be branded under our agency's name? +

Yes, we offer white-label partnerships. Your clients see only your agency's brand — we work behind the scenes. We provide all the sales materials you need: itineraries, photos and descriptions in Arabic and English.

Can you create Ramadan programmes? +

Yes. We create special Ramadan programmes: Suhoor and Iftar at restaurants with views of the main landmarks, adapted tour schedules around prayer times, and night walks through Moscow and St. Petersburg.
