About Insiders

Built from a dream.
Run with a purpose.

At 16, the founder had one dream: build a hotel, live on the top floor, and welcome the world. He studied airport management, worked in restaurants and hotels, and somewhere in those years understood hospitality at its simplest: people travel to be happy, make memories, and come back.

That understanding became INSIDERS Tourism — a Dubai DMC built not on templates, but on genuine care. We create programmes from scratch, respond faster than anyone in the industry, and own our fleet and guides so nothing is ever outsourced. Every detail, from arrival to farewell, is our responsibility.

The hotel is still the dream. To reach it, we're building 8–10 INSIDERS destinations worldwide. We're already on our way.

  • All 7 Emirates, plus Oman, Jordan & Bahrain
  • 4 global markets: Latin America, Russia, UK & USA
  • Available 24/7 — from first enquiry to final goodbye

When Things Go Wrong

The night we turned
a crisis into a memory.

In 2022, we were managing a large-scale corporate event for McDonald's at Atlantis The Palm. The hotel had made a booking error — the ballroom we needed had been given to another client on the same setup day. Neither side could move. Neither side was at fault. But one side was going to have a worse night than they planned.

We chose to make sure it wasn't ours.

We called the other client — a German group for a digital currency company — and offered to build their AV setup for them: lights, stage, sound, LED screens. The same full production we had planned for McDonald's. At no cost to them.

The hotel agreed to cover our compensation. The German group got a setup bigger than anything they'd planned. And McDonald's had one of the best events in their company's history — and came back to us the following year.

"That is how we work. When something goes wrong, we don't explain — we solve."

— INSIDERS Tourism
