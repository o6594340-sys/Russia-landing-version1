01 — Strategy

Why we design against the norm

Every competitor in UAE tourism uses the same visual formula. The new Insiders Tourism website breaks that pattern deliberately — and turns it into a competitive advantage.

The Core Idea

"Every competitor says luxury with black and gold.
Insiders will say luxury with light and space."

Every other UAE tourism site

  • Black or dark navy background
  • Gold accents
  • Dense, busy layouts
  • Feature lists and bullet points
  • Generic stock photography
  • Looks like every other brand

New Insiders Tourism

  • Warm white & mallow backgrounds
  • Muted magenta — from the logo
  • Spacious, editorial layout
  • Short, confident statements
  • Authentic UAE imagery
  • Instantly recognisable

04 — Page Structure

Nine sections.
One clear journey.

The homepage guides the visitor from first impression to enquiry — each section has a single job.

01 Header & Navigation Sticky. Transparent over hero, white on scroll. Logo left, menu right, "Book Now" always visible.
02 Hero Full-screen photo of Dubai at dusk. Large serif headline with italic accent. Two call-to-action buttons.
03 Stats 4 animated counters: years in business · travellers served · emirates covered · experiences offered.
04 Services 4 cards: City Tours · Desert Safaris · Transfers & Chauffeur · MICE & Groups. Each with icon, description and link.
05 Featured Tours 3 top experiences with photo, description, duration tag and starting price. "View All" link below.
06 About Photo left, text right. Key message: "We live here. That's the difference." Company values and credentials.
07 Reviews 3 real guest quotes with name and country. TripAdvisor "Excellent" rating badge.
08 Contact & Enquiry Contact info left, enquiry form right. Phone · Email · Office. Form: name, email, service, message.
09 Footer Logo, navigation links, social media icons, copyright. Dark background (#1E1A1C).

06 — Required from Insiders Tourism

To complete the website

The design and code are ready. The following content is needed from the client to finalise the site.

Hero photograph

High-quality UAE landscape — Dubai skyline at dusk, Burj Al Arab, or desert sunset. Minimum 2400 × 1600 px.

3 tour photographs

One per featured tour: Dubai City Tour · Desert Safari · Abu Dhabi. Minimum 1200 × 900 px each.

About / team photograph

Team portrait or atmospheric UAE scene. Minimum 1000 × 1250 px (portrait orientation).

Logo file

PNG or SVG with transparent background. Both colour and white versions if available.

Confirmed statistics

Years in business · total clients or travellers served · number of tour experiences offered.

Tour prices

Starting price in AED for the 3 featured tours. "From AED ___" format.

3 guest reviews

Real quotes from satisfied travellers with first name and country. Or we pull directly from TripAdvisor.

Copy review & approval

All text has been written — client to review section by section and confirm or request changes.
