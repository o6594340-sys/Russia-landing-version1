## VISA — JORDANIAN & LEVANT PASSPORTS

Most Jordanian, Lebanese, and Palestinian passport holders qualify for the Russian **e-visa** (single-entry tourist, up to 16 days).

**Process:**
1. Client applies online at [`evisa.kdmid.ru`](https://evisa.kdmid.ru)
2. We provide the invitation letter and instructions with each booking
3. Processing time: typically 4 working days
4. Cost: approx. $50–60 (government fee, paid by applicant)

*Always verify current visa requirements for the specific passport before confirming the booking. Visa policy can change.*
